Michigan Tap Water Alert

What's Really in Your Tap Water?

Michigan tap water contains dozens of contaminants that affect your hormones, brain, cardiovascular health, and development. Many are legal — but that doesn't mean they're safe.

Chlorine & Chloramines
Added to municipal water as a disinfectant. Reacts with organic matter to form trihalomethanes (THMs) — known carcinogens. Also kills beneficial gut bacteria.
Health EffectsDisrupts gut flora, linked to bladder & rectal cancers, skin irritation, respiratory issues, thyroid suppression.
Fluoride
Deliberately added to Michigan water at 0.7 mg/L. Accumulates in the pineal gland and bones. Recent NIH studies raise serious concerns about effects on IQ development in children.
Health EffectsNeurotoxicity in children, pineal gland calcification, dental/skeletal fluorosis, thyroid disruption, reduced IQ scores.
Estrogen & Birth Control
Synthetic estrogens from birth control pills pass through sewage treatment essentially intact. Measurable levels found in Michigan drinking water sources.
Health EffectsLowered testosterone in men, feminizing effects, disrupted puberty in children, reduced sperm count, hormonal cancers.
Testosterone Disruptors
A cocktail of endocrine-disrupting chemicals — atrazine, phthalates, BPA, and pharmaceutical residues — act as xeno-estrogens, suppressing androgen production.
Health EffectsLowered testosterone, infertility, weight gain, fatigue, depression, loss of muscle mass, reduced libido.
Lead & Heavy Metals
Michigan's aging infrastructure leaches lead, copper, manganese, and arsenic. Even homes far from Flint can have lead service lines or brass fixtures leaching metals.
Health EffectsIrreversible brain damage in children. All heavy metals impair kidneys, raise blood pressure, and cause neurotoxicity.
PFAS "Forever Chemicals"
Michigan has the highest known PFAS contamination sites in the US — 200+ locations. These man-made chemicals don't break down and accumulate in body tissues.
Health EffectsThyroid disease, immune suppression, hormonal cancers, fertility problems, elevated cholesterol, childhood developmental delays.
Nitrates & Pesticides
Agricultural runoff from Michigan farms introduces nitrates and herbicides like atrazine into groundwater — especially relevant for Mt. Pleasant residents.
Health EffectsAtrazine disrupts hormones at very low concentrations. Nitrates cause "blue baby syndrome" and are linked to thyroid and ovarian cancers.
Pharmaceutical Residues
Antibiotics, antidepressants, blood pressure medications, and chemotherapy drugs are regularly detected in municipal water. Treatment plants are not designed to remove them.
Health EffectsAntibiotic resistance, mood disruption, cardiovascular effects, and long-term cumulative toxicity from micro-dose exposure.
Microplastics & Nanoplastics
Plastic particles are now found in virtually every water source including the Great Lakes. Nanoplastics have been detected crossing the blood-brain barrier and in human blood.
Health EffectsInflammation, endocrine disruption, cellular toxicity, potential carcinogenesis; long-term research is still emerging but alarming.
Our Technology

10-Stage Reverse Osmosis
Filtration System

Our commercial-grade RO system eliminates over 99% of dissolved solids, contaminants, and impurities. Each stage targets specific threats.

01
Sediment Pre-Filter
Removes rust, dirt, sand, and particulate matter 5 microns and larger.
02
Granular Activated Carbon
Adsorbs chlorine, chloramines, VOCs, and improves taste and odor.
03
Carbon Block Filter
Second-stage carbon filtration for remaining organics and fine sediment down to 1 micron.
04
RO Membrane
Blocks 95–99% of dissolved salts, heavy metals, fluoride, nitrates, and pharmaceuticals at the molecular level.
05
Post-Carbon Polishing
Final carbon stage polishes purified water, removing any residual taste or odor.
06
Deionization (DI)
Ion exchange resin removes remaining dissolved ions, achieving ultra-pure water near 0 TDS.
07
Alkaline Remineralization
Beneficial minerals (calcium, magnesium, potassium) reintroduced for proper hydration and healthy pH.
08
Infrared Energy Filter
Far-infrared rays energize water molecules, improving cell absorption for deeper hydration.
09
Magnetic Ionization
Magnetic field treatment restructures water molecules for improved bioavailability and antioxidant properties.
10
UV Sterilization
Ultraviolet light eliminates 99.99% of remaining bacteria, viruses, and microorganisms without chemicals.
